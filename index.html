Point #12 (official wording):

“WE BELIEVE that Allah (God) appeared in the Person of Master W. Fard Muhammad, July, 1930; the long-awaited ‘Messiah’ of the Christians and the ‘Mahdi’ of the Muslims.

Meaning Parallels: Point #12 mapped across the 3 other systems

Point #12 is a doctrine of divine appearance in history for the restoration of peace/order. In the other systems, that same “arrival of guidance” appears as oracle, science of balance, and resurrection training.

Universal Meaning Ifá Ayurveda Metu Neter (Ausar–Auset) NOI — Point #12
Divine Guidance enters time Orunmila = witness of destiny; guidance through oracle and Orí alignment. Rishis preserve life-science; guidance through dharma + health law. Auset recovers the “hidden name” (higher knowledge) to restore Ausar (order). Allah appears in person as Master W. Fard Muhammad for end-time restoration.
Restoration before peace Right character (Ìwà Pẹ̀lẹ́) restores relationship with Orí and community. Balance of doshas restores stability; disorder produces disease. Ma’at restores harmony; resurrection is the repair of inner disorder. Universal government of peace follows divine intervention + correction.
Discipline is required Taboos/ritual discipline protect Aṣẹ and destiny execution. Daily regimen (dinacharya) protects Agni and mind clarity. Spiritual cultivation disciplines the lower nature to awaken higher mind. Belief is not passive—Point #12 is treated as foundation requiring proof/defense.
Inner change precedes outer change Orí correction precedes success in the world. Digestive/mind balance precedes strong health outcomes. Inner resurrection precedes liberation from Set (lower chaos). Divine appearance sets the stage for transformed society and peace.
